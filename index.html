Yangsu Liu

I am looking forward to research jobs now. I received my Ph.D. degree from Department of Computer Science and Engineering, Shanghai Jiao Tong University in June, 2023, where I was co-advised by Prof. Guihai Chen and Prof. Fan Wu. Email:liu_yangsu@sjtu.edu.cn  /  DBLP

Research

My research interests are in the intersection of economics, learning and networking. In particular, I have focused on Data Trading, Online Advertising, Federated Learning using Algorithmic Game Theory.
